About CAZZPrep

Built by students, for students

CAZZPrep is a student-driven platform designed to simplify access to Previous Year Question Papers and academic exam resources in one organized space.

Coverage

Class Routines, Syllabus & branch-wise PYQs

Purpose

Cazz the SEM

Our goal

Reduce the time students spend searching for PYQs across random groups, PDFs and chats — and instead provide a clean, fast and structured academic archive that keeps growing with new subjects, updated papers and smarter features.
